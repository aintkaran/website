About me
Have you heard of the belif especially well established in Indian middle class families of "When confused, choose engineering"? Well, let's just say Karan fell victim to that notion and it changed his life for the best.
Karan, being born in a small town, had to move out to aquire basic schooling. From Delhi to Kerala, Bihar to Rajasthan, all this movement did one thing and that is make him more aware of the cultural differences but more importantly the esscence of what binds us together. In the midst of all the cultural chaos and uncertainity, he found his companion in perfectly imperfect music.
He didn't end up join engineering, instead took some time off and completed his Diploma in the Art of Photography, tried his hands on businesses and trades, and is now pursuing User Experience Design. His passion to learn drives him to take on random colourful adventures deep into diverse subject matters. His borderline neurodivergence also contributes to his devotion towards quality craftsmanship.
He loves to take some time off cycling through his, now, suburban town taking in all that he missed growing away from home.
